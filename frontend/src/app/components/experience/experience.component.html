During my time at Monogram I worked on developing powerful software integrations for Monogram's main product: the Monogram Creative Console — a modular peripheral device aimed toward creative professionals (check it out on the Monogram website). The Monogram Creative Console provides superior functionality over typical HID devices by providing direct software integrations into many professional applications — some examples include many Adobe cloud applications (Premier, Photoshop, Lightroom, etc...) and Unreal Engine (for real-time virtual production). My role during this work term is best describes as a full stack developer — I worked on the desktop application, the desktop service, the Adobe integrations and the Unreal Engine integration.   • five deployment types: build, sign, preview, stage, deploy
